We collect only the necessary information to provide you with a smooth learning experience:

We provide paid courses and e-books. For payments, we use secure third-party processors:

3. Third-Party Services

To make the app functional, we use trusted services:

We keep your data as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account or any personal information, you can contact us at the email provided below.

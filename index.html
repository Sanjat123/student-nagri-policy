Account and Data Deletion (User Rights)

Google Play requires developers to provide a way for users to delete their accounts both within the app and online.

1. How to Delete Your Account:

  • In-App: Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account. This will permanently remove your data.
  • Web Request: You can submit a deletion request via our Account Deletion Portal.
  • Email: Contact us at support@studentnagri.com with the subject "Delete My Account".

2. Data Removed Upon Deletion: All personal info, course progress, and login credentials will be wiped from our Firebase database within 7 days of the request.
